The mass-energy equivalence formula is given by:

\begin{equation}
E = mc^2
\end{equation}

Where:
\begin{itemize}
    \item $E$ is energy,
    \item $m$ is mass,
    \item $c$ is the speed of light in a vacuum.
\end{itemize}
